---
title: Manual Entry (the Bucket Approach)
description: Enter your account totals directly in the Setup Panel
---

Manual Entry puts you in control: you add up your balances by account type and enter the totals directly in the Setup Panel. No app subscriptions, no CSV exports, nothing to configure beyond the numbers you already know.

**Best for:** first-time users, quick what-if scenarios, users without an app subscription like Monarch.

## The Five BucketsSection titled "The Five Buckets"

Your net worth is made of different "buckets." For Manual Entry, add up the balances in each type and enter the totals:

Bucket
What Goes Here
Example

**Cash**
Checking, savings, money market accounts
$45,000

**Taxable brokerage**
Regular investment accounts (not retirement)
$120,000

**Traditional IRA / 401(k)**
Pre-tax retirement accounts
$380,000

**Roth IRA / 401(k)**
After-tax retirement accounts
$95,000

**Home value**
What your home is approximately worth today
$620,000

**Liabilities**
Mortgages, car loans, other debts
$393,000

Don't worry about perfect categorization — you can change it later.
