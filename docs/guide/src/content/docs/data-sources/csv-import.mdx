title: CSV Import
description: Upload a CSV export of your accounts
---

CSV Import lets you load account balances from a CSV file. The importer reads your export, groups rows by account name, keeps the latest balance for each account, and presents them for classification in the Setup Panel.

**Best for:** users who want a one-time Monarch data snapshot without running the MCP server, who use other apps with compatible exports, or who want full control over account classification.

## Getting the CSVSection titled "Getting the CSV"

**Monarch Money users:** Open the **Accounts** page and click **Download CSV**. The export contains a day-by-day historical record of every account's balance — from the earliest data Monarch has for you through today.

**Other apps:** If your app can export to CSV with columns named `Date`, `Account`, and `Balance`, you can import it directly. If the column names differ, rename them in a spreadsheet before importing.

## Required FormatSection titled "Required Format"

Your CSV must have these three columns in the header row:

Column
Description
Example

`Date`
Balance snapshot date (YYYY-MM-DD)
`2026-01-15`

`Account`
Name of the account
`Vanguard Roth IRA`

`Balance`
Account balance — positive for assets, negative for debts
`45230.50`

A sample CSV is available at [docs/assets/sample-accounts.csv](https://github.com/LemurTech/Net-Worth-Navigator/blob/main/docs/assets/sample-accounts.csv) in the repository.

## How It WorksSection titled "How It Works"

The importer:

- Reads the CSV header to find `Date`, `Account`, and `Balance` columns
- Groups rows by `Account` name
- Keeps the **latest** `Date`'s `Balance` for each account
- Presents the parsed accounts for you to classify by category (Cash, Taxable, Traditional IRA, Roth, etc.) and owner

**How it works:** The importer reads the CSV, groups rows by `Account` name, and keeps the latest `Date`'s `Balance` for each account. A sample CSV is available at [docs/assets/sample-accounts.csv](https://github.com/LemurTech/Net-Worth-Navigator/blob/main/docs/assets/sample-accounts.csv).

![CSV import preview in the Setup Panel showing the file upload, parsed account list with category and owner dropdowns, and Source column](/Net-Worth-Navigator/nwn-csv-import-preview.png)

To use: In the Setup Panel's **Accounts** tab, select **CSV Import** as your data source, upload your CSV file, review the parsed accounts, and click **Import & Save**.

**How often to re-import?**

As often as you export from your source — monthly or quarterly. Re-importing preserves your previous classifications, so new imports only need attention for new or changed accounts.

**How balance updates work:** The `Date` column in the CSV is used to pick the latest snapshot for each account — the date itself is then discarded. The resulting balances are used as the starting point at your scenario's configured `start_year`, regardless of when they were captured. For best accuracy, set `start_year` close to the date of your latest CSV data.
