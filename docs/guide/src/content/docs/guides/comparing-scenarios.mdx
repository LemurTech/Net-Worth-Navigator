title: Comparing Scenarios
description: Side-by-side comparison of multiple financial plans
---

The Compare Page lets you overlay multiple scenarios on the same charts, view delta (difference) against a baseline, and compare key metrics side by side.

## Opening the Compare PageSection titled "Opening the Compare Page"

- From the **Projection Shell**, click **Compare Scenarios** in the toolbar
- Direct URL: `http://localhost:8010/compare.html`
- Deep-link with URL params: `compare.html?a=scenario_a&b=scenario_b&mode=deterministic`

## Selecting ScenariosSection titled "Selecting Scenarios"

- Click scenario chips at the top of the page to toggle them on/off
- At least two scenarios must be selected for the comparison to render
- The first selected scenario is treated as the **baseline** for delta calculations
- Switch between Deterministic, Historical, and Monte Carlo modes using the mode selector

## What You SeeSection titled "What You See"
### Total Net Worth ChartSection titled "Total Net Worth Chart"

Overlays each scenario's net worth trajectory on a single chart. Each scenario gets a stable colour:

- Blue — first selected scenario
- Red — second scenario
- Purple — third scenario
- (More colours cycle for additional scenarios)

### Investable Portfolio ChartSection titled "Investable Portfolio Chart"

Same overlay comparison, but showing only investable assets (taxable, traditional IRA, Roth) — excluding cash and home equity.

### Net Worth Delta ChartSection titled "Net Worth Delta Chart"

Shows the difference between each non-baseline scenario and the baseline. This is useful for answering questions like *"How much more would I have at retirement if I started contributing earlier?"* or *"What's the cost of retiring two years later?"*

### KPI TableSection titled "KPI Table"

Side-by-side metrics for each selected scenario:

Metric
What It Shows

Starting Net Worth
Year-0 net worth

Net Worth at Retirement
Net worth in the retirement year of the first-retiring person

Retirement Age
Age at retirement

End Net Worth
Net worth at the end of the projection

Ending Year
The final year of the projection

Rows are highlighted with **green** (positive) or **red** (negative) delta indicators showing how each scenario differs from the baseline.

### Monte Carlo ModeSection titled "Monte Carlo Mode"

When viewing Monte Carlo mode, additional metrics appear:

Metric
What It Shows

Probability of Success
% of simulations that don't fail

Worst-Decile Terminal NW
The 10th percentile ending net worth

P10 / P25 / P50 / P75 / P90
Terminal net worth percentile bands

Peak Failure Rate
Highest % of simulations failing in a single year

Median First Failure
The earliest year where failure occurs in the median case

## TipsSection titled "Tips"

- The compare page reads sidecar CSV data, so scenarios must have been rendered at least once with the current version
- Use **Deterministic** mode for cleanest comparison between scenarios — random variation in Monte Carlo can obscure small differences
- Both **Sample A** and **Sample B** are designed as an A/B comparison pair
- **Deep-link** a specific comparison via URL: `compare.html?a=scenario_a&b=scenario_b&mode=deterministic`
